Simple checks for safer driving

Routine maintenance helps prevent breakdowns, improves handling, and keeps you legal.

  • Tires: check pressure, tread depth and condition regularly.
  • Lights: ensure headlights, indicators and brake lights all work correctly.
  • Fluids: monitor oil, coolant, brake fluid and screenwash levels.

Good maintenance habits support safer journeys for drivers, passengers and everyone around you.

Brakes and steering

Respond immediately to unusual noises, vibrations or stiffness in the steering wheel.

Service schedule

Follow the manufacturer’s service intervals and get annual MOT checks on time.

Maintenance habits

  1. Inspect your car before every long journey.
  2. Keep records of services, repairs and checks.
  3. Fix small issues quickly before they become bigger problems.

Good car maintenance leads to better performance and safer travel for everyone.
