UK road safety by the numbers

Understanding the latest statistics helps make safer choices.

  • Road deaths: thousands of lives are lost every year on UK roads.
  • Injuries: tens of thousands of people are seriously injured annually.
  • Young drivers: new drivers are disproportionately affected in collisions.

What the figures tell us

These trends are a reminder that safer behaviour saves lives.

  1. Drive within limits and adapt to conditions.
  2. Keep your vehicle maintained and legal.
  3. Support learners and vulnerable road users with care.

For full datasets and regional reports, consult GOV.UK or your local authority; use these summaries to prioritise safer choices.

Even small improvements in behaviour make a big difference to UK road safety.
